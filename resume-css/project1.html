Description

Hidden Code is an outreach initiative created to address the lack of girls selecting Computer Science GCSEs and pursuing technology-related career paths. The project involved visiting secondary schools and delivering interactive sessions for Year 9 students (ages 13–14) to introduce digital career options and build confidence through beginner-friendly computer science activities.

Skills Learned

  • Demonstrated leadership and initiative through planning and delivering school outreach sessions
  • Developed communication skills by explaining technical ideas clearly to beginners
  • Strengthened teamwork and collaboration while organizing activities and engaging with students
  • Gained foundational experience in web development and programming (HTML, Python)

Quick Details

  • Audience: Year 9 students (13–14)
  • Focus: Digital careers + confidence building
  • Format: Interactive workshops
